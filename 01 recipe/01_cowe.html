omelete recipe

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • cheese
  • milk
  • green pepper
  • red onion
  • butter

steps

  1. crack and whisk eggs
  2. add 2 tbsp milk
  3. grate cheese
  4. slice veggies
  5. put butter in pan with heat on low
  6. pour eggs into pan
  7. when eggs are almost cooked, add veggies and cheese to half
  8. fold eggs in half
  9. remove from pan
